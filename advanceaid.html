Advanced First Aid Training

Comprehensive first aid training including CPR certification, wound care, and emergency medical response skills essential for crisis situations.

CPR Basics

Learn the critical steps of CPR: 30 chest compressions followed by 2 rescue breaths. Push hard and fast at a rhythm of 100-120 compressions per minute to maximize survival chances in cardiac emergencies.

Wound Care and Bleeding Control

Master bleeding control techniques including applying direct pressure, elevating wounds if possible, and the proper use of tourniquets only in life-threatening scenarios.

Emergency Medical Response

Understand when and how to call emergency services, provide clear location and symptom details, and protocols to assist victims until professional help arrives.
